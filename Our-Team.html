Meet Our Team

About YSD

Across the UK, we support individuals to build their career management, work-based and employability skills, throughout their career journey, from school/college/universities, into further learning opportunities and employment. We are able to use skills intelligence to help inform the decisions of people across the globe, at all stages of their careers, so that they are well-placed to take up the opportunities arising within the economy. We target delivery of this support to those who need it most, ensuring all individuals are enabled to achieve their full potential. We , at YSD | YOUTH SKILLS DEVELOPMENT | , are a team of professional educators with decades of experience as teachers, trainers, administrators, curriculum planners, and professors. This depth and breadth of experience allows us to support all members of the educational community, from administrators to parents, bringing hands-on, hands-on experience to counseling practice. They are dedicated to working with schools and teachers and have extensive experience in raising standards, rigor and student expectations.

About Our CEO

Founder of YSD Education and Technology Consultancy with a passion for improving Educational Technology globally Dr Ali Imam has led the Digital and Emerging Technology Sector within the UK headquarters of BIG4 consultancy firm along with experience in the government sectors including Oil & Gas, Healthcare and Nuclear Engineering as a Senior Nuclear Scientist for over 10 years striving for the best. He is also an active Adjunct Professor within several UK Universities. Dr Ali has completed his PhD in Engineering and has a wealth of experience designing and delivering technology in the industry and academia and has spent over 6 years working with, and successfully trading with the education sectors in various countries. His trade relationships extend into Europe, Southeast Asia and the Middle East, which provides him valuable insight into the field of Educational Consultancy all over the world.
